Mission

ZOTHEOS exists to push back on a world where information is often shaped by incentives that don’t serve people: attention economies, algorithmic manipulation, and centralized control. The long-term goal is an ethical intelligence system that helps humans think clearly, learn quickly, and build a better future — without surrendering privacy or autonomy.

Why offline matters Offline capability protects access during outages, censorship, instability, or paywall barriers.
Why “ethical” matters Because intelligence without values becomes a weapon. Alignment isn’t optional.
Why “fusion” matters Multiple perspectives reduce single-model blind spots and improve balance.
