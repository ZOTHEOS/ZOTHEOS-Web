Why ZOTHEOS exists

The internet used to feel like a library. Now it often feels like a casino. ZOTHEOS is my push in the opposite direction: a calm tool for learning and decision-making that doesn’t need your data to “work.”

The long-term goal is simple: keep knowledge accessible, keep people in control, and keep the mission independent.
