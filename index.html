Why ZOTHEOS exists

Most systems today compete for attention. They optimize for engagement, not understanding. ZOTHEOS is built for the opposite outcome: clear, calm intelligence that helps people learn and act without being tracked, nudged, or profiled.

The mission is simple: keep knowledge accessible, keep privacy intact, and keep the tool reliable even when the internet isn’t. Over time, this becomes something bigger than a product — it becomes infrastructure for human clarity.
